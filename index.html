About Us

Who We Are

SCC is a software, cloud and consulting company located in Blumenau, Santa Catarina, the 'Silicon Valley' of Brazil. Founded by Wolfgang Unger with experience in Software Development, Architecture and Consulting of about 20 years in Munich Germany in projects for big enterprises and medium sized companies

What We Do

We are focused on 3 major areas, software development, cloud architecture & engineering and consulting & training. Agile software development, full stack from frontend to backend. Offshore Development for german, european and american customers but also for local customers in Brazil. Cloud technologies, focused on Amazon Web Services but also Azure and more, for your transition into the cloud. Consulting and Coaching in all IT areas to support your digital business transformation

Why Choose Us

Based on an outstanding experience in big software projects for enterprise as well as small companies, we guarantee high professional solutions for your company with german efficiency and quality. We are the perfect shoring partner for european and american customers as well as your local IT partner in Santa Catarina and Brazil.
